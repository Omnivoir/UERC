🔥 Structure Fire Operations

Size-up & Risk Assessment (SOP 900.44)
  • Establish command & location.
  • Identify type of structure, occupancy, exposures.
  • Check water supply & hydrant locations.
  • Review pre-plans & building construction.
Incident Command & ICS
  • Follow NIMS ICS: Command, Operations, Logistics, Planning, Finance.
  • Establish divisions/zones for attack, search, ventilation.
  • Ensure accountability & PAR every 15 min.
Fire Attack & Tactics
  • Use appropriate hoseline: 1¾” or 2½” depending on flow requirements.
  • Coordinate ventilation prior to interior attack.
  • Maintain backup line; ensure rapid intervention team on standby.

☣️ Hazardous Materials Response

ERG Guidance & Isolation
  • Identify product via placard/ERG book.
  • Isolate area per initial isolation distances.
  • Don appropriate PPE level (full ensemble, SCBA).
Mitigation & Decontamination (SOP 1000.02)
  • Establish hot, warm, cold zones.
  • Use dry decon or water if compatible.
  • Collect runoff; prevent environmental contamination.

🚑 Emergency Medical Services

Adult Cardiac Arrest (SOP 1300.1)
  • High-quality CPR: 100-120 cpm, 5-6 cm depth.
  • Minimize interruptions; rotate every 2 min.
  • Use AED ASAP; follow shock/no-shock protocol.
  • Post-ROSC: maintain SBP > 90 mmHg, SpO₂ > 94%.
Check DNR before initiating care (SOP 1300.4).
Stroke Assessment & Transport (SOP 1300.3)
  • Perform FAST; record last known well.
  • Measure glucose; rule out hypoglycemia.
  • Notify stroke center; maintain SBP < 185 mmHg.
Pediatric Care (SOP 1300.9)
  • Use Broselow tape; weight-based dosing.
  • Compressions one-third chest depth; 100-120/min.
  • IO access if IV > 90 s unsuccessful.
Airway Management (NREMT/KBEMS)
  • OPA/NPA adjuncts; two-person BVM with 100% O₂.
  • ETCO₂ confirmation; aim 35-45 mmHg.
  • RSI protocols: etomidate, succinylcholine per medical control.
DNR & End-of-Life (SOP 1300.4)
  • Validate DNR form; consult medical control.
  • Document confirmation source & time in PCR.

🚒 Pump Operations

Engine Pumping & Pressure
  • Calculate intake pressure: engine pressure + static intake.
  • Select nozzle pressure: 100 psi fog, 50 psi standard.
  • Account for friction loss: 2 psi per 100 ft at 500 gpm.
Mobile Water Supply
  • Relay pumping: use tandem, mid-point, or mobile relay.
  • Monitor water tank levels; refill via hydrant or tanker shuttle.

🛗 Aerial Operations

Ladder Placement & Stabilization
  • Establish level base; use outriggers fully extended.
  • Position ladder 75° (1:4 ratio) from ground to tip.
Tip Load & Safety
  • Verify manufacturer tip load; limit personnel/tools.
  • Maintain three points of contact climbing.

🤝 Mutual Aid Procedures (SOP 900.21)

Request & Deployment
  • Activate mutual aid channels via dispatch.
  • Use standardized ICS 205 communications plan.
  • Document resource arrival & assignment in ICS forms.
